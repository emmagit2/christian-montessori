ours core value

Discipline

Recognizing our duty to instill discipline, we go beyond academics. Our focus on moral values and life skills has established us as a beacon of excellence. We are proud to be synonymous with a holistic education that molds students into well-rounded individuals, ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.

Honesty

In our school,We believe in uprightness, fairness, sincerity, and truthfulness. We are always ready to defend our values and what we represent. honesty is more than a policy; it's a value we cultivate. In our vibrant learning community, we inspire a culture of integrity, fostering an environment where students thrive academically and personally

God fearing

At Christian Montessori School, our commitment is deeply rooted in the guidance of God. We strive to instill values that go beyond academics, fostering a holistic education where each child not only learns but also grows in the light of Christian principles. In this nurturing environment, we believe that a strong foundation in faith enriches the educational journey for every student.

Obedience

We impart essential lessons on obedience, educating children on the rewards of respecting parents, educators, leaders, the government, and other influential figures. Our commitment to instilling these values ensures that our students not only excel academically but also grow into responsible and respectful individuals who contribute positively to society

Creativity

Christian Montessori School nurtures creativity in a supportive environment, blending Christian values and Montessori principles. Our approach emphasizes personalized learning, hands-on experiences, and encourages exploration, questioning, and creative self-expression for each child.

Love

As a top-notch school, our foundation is built on the profound principle of love. Rooted in Christian values, we prioritize creating an environment where love is not just a concept but a lived reality. This commitment extends to every child, fostering empathy, kindness, and an appreciation for individual uniqueness. In this atmosphere of love, we believe each child can thrive academically, socially, and spiritually.
